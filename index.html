A Sanctuary of Luxury & Self-Care

Velvet Bloom Salon — Bistupur, Jamshedpur

Est. 2016, Velvet Bloom has grown to become the premier wellness retreat and beauty destination for ladies in Jamshedpur. Strategically nestled along Bistupur Main Road, our parlour is designed to isolate you completely from the hustle of the city, cocooning you in an elegant candle-lit, lavender-scented sanctuary.

We strongly believe that self-care is a ritual. That is why our elite therapists and stylists work exclusively with standard international beauty brands like L'Oréal, Schwarzkopf, and OPI, customizing each facial, haircut, and manicure to bring out your unique, radiating bloom.
